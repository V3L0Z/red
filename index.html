About Red Events

Red Events is a small business that started in 2018 in Norwalk, CA. We began from our home with just a few Chiavari chairs and have gradually grown with the help of my children and their high school friends. Despite facing challenges like the coronavirus, inflation, and a partnership change, we have persevered and built strong relationships with our clients and vendors. These experiences have shaped us into the company we are today. Our main focus is ensuring 100% client satisfaction, and our dedicated staff works hard to perfect each event and create lasting customer relationships. We strive to excel in the entertainment and event industry by providing our clients with our best work, positive attitude, and excellent customer service. At Red Events, we treat each event as if it were our own, paying attention to every detail. We are committed to being there for our clients and their families, no matter the time, weather, or distance. Contact us today for your next event.
